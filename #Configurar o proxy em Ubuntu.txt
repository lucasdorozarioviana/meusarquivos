#Configurar o proxy em Ubuntu. 

A resposta demorava e nao concluia. Para testar se as configuraçoes de proxy existem, digitar no shell os dois comandos um de cada vez:
env
export

Se nao existem, editar 3 arquivos:
/etc/environment
/etc/apt/apt.conf
/etc/profile

nano /etc/environment
http_proxy=http://enderecoproxy:8080/
https_proxy=http://enderecoproxy:8080/
ftp_proxy=http://enderecoproxy:8080/

nano /etc/apt/apt.conf
Acquire::http::Proxy "http://enderecoproxy:8080";
Acquire::https::Proxy "http://enderecoproxy:8080";
Acquire::ftp::Proxy "http://enderecoproxy:8080";

nano /etc/profile
acrescentar ao final do arquivo:
http_proxy="http://enderecoproxy:8080"
https_proxy="http://enderecoproxy:8080"
ftp_proxy="http://enderecoproxy:8080"
export http_proxy https_proxy ftp_proxy

No Ubuntu 18.04 original o arquivo com as configuraçoes de proxy fica no:
/etc/apt/apt.conf.d/90curtin-aptproxy
